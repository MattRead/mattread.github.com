SS Edmund Fitzgerald

Published November 10th, 2005

❦

SS Edmund Fitzgerald was a ship that sank suddenly on Lake Superior, November 10, 1975. The ship went down without a distress signal in a November gale. It sank in 530 feet (162 m) of water at 46°59.9′ N 85°6.6′ W, in Canadian waters about 17 statute miles (15 nm; 27 km) from the entrance to Whitefish Bay. All 29 members of the crew were killed.

29 souls not forgotten.
