Ottawa Blues Festival

Published July 13th, 2004

❦

The Ottawa Blues Fest is on this week, with a lot of great names; Brian Adams, Colin James, Blue Rodeo, and the list goes on. This Friday (July 16th) I will be going to see Blue Rodeo play on the main stage. I hope the weather cooperates, and the beer goes down good, cause I’m looking forward to a good time. Ahhh, I can’t wait to see all those topless chick’s on Sparks Street.
