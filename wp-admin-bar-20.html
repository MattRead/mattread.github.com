WP Admin Bar 2.2

Published March 11th, 2005

❦

This plugin will add a bar at the top of your template file with links to the admin sections of WordPress. However it will only add this bar for users that are logged in.

WP Admin Bar 2.2 was written by Matt Read. Matt is the owner of this site and primary author. He also tweets, posts photos, stars in videos, listens to music, makes software, develops Habari, and creates websites.
