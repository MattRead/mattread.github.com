GMail is Here!

Published June 21st, 2004

❦

YES! I just got an invite to GMail, Google’s new email service with 1 GB of storage. I don’t know how I’m ever going to use up that much space, but I guess I’ll never have to delete email again. I haven’t had much time to play with it yet, but I plan on telling you all about it so stay tuned. Goodbye Yahoo, hello GMail.

One big problem I have with it already, is that they don’t support Konqueror. I know that it is still in the testing stage, but I would really like to see them support Konqueror. At least they support Firefox

Thanks to Kenneth for the invite (KentVille)
