#chill, An IRC Channel

Published December 23rd, 2004

❦

So you’ve been in that “support” channel on IRC, and someone didn’t appreciate your sly comments. Well then, welcome #chill into your life then. Okay, so what is #chill? It’s an IRC channel on Freenode that is a place to relax, sit back, and say what ever the hell you want to. As Korgan, it’s creator, says, please join #chill and just talk about whatever it is you feel like talking about.

So I hope to see you all very soon in #chill. I hear there’s lots of naked chicks and a pretty cool bot there too.
