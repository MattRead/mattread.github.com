Silly Word Day

Published March 17th, 2005

❦

Twidget has proclaimed that Thursday, March 31st is “Silly Word Day”. I have been tying to come up with a word but have not yet found one. Oh, what is this? well here’s an excerpt from his site.

On that day, you are encouraged to participate in the humorous use of the English language by making the post that features or otherwise highlights a silly English word.

Go read his post for all the rules/guidelines. Now back to finding my Silly Word.

Update: I have chosen the word “dabble”.
