SafeSurf Rated

Published October 14th, 2004

❦

My site is now rated with the SafeSurf rating system. It’s rated as “Adult Supervision Recommended”. I haven’t been approved by SafeSurf yet, but hopefully I soon will get my license. Have a look at my rating.

SafeSurf is voluntary rating service which uses the PICS specification to rate web pages based on content, using meta tags. I highly recommend you rate your site today.
